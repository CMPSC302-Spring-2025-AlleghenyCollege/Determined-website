About Determined

Stay strong. Stay sober. Will you?

Determined is an interactive digital experience that puts you in the shoes of someone recovering from substance use disorder (SUD). The goal is to keep your "resist" points higher than your "use" points as you navigate everyday challenges, tough decisions, and unexpected events over the course of a year.

You'll face real-world scenarios‚Äîboth good and bad‚Äîand make choices that shape your recovery journey. Each choice impacts your chances of relapse, which occurs if your "use" points get too high compared to your "resist" points.

Recovery Tools

When times get tough, you can use various tools to boost your resistance. These include:

  • Breathing exercises to stay calm in stressful situations
  • Attending support group meetings for encouragement
  • Calling a sponsor when you need immediate support
  • Physical exercise to improve mental health
  • Mindfulness practices to stay present and aware

Determined is about building empathy and understanding how difficult, complex, and courageous the recovery journey really is.
